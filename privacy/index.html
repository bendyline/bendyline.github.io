Privacy Policy

Some ways we use the usage data include operating, improving and personalizing the Services.

Cookies & Other Information

The Service uses "cookies", which are small text files placed on a device's hard disk by a web server. We may use both session and persistent cookies and similar technologies to better understand how you interact with our services, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on our services, and to improve our services. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by editing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit.

Any information you provide on those sites is provided directly to the owner of that site and is subject to that party's privacy policy. Our privacy policy does not apply to such sites, and we are not responsible for the content or privacy and security practices and policies of those sites.
